Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my reminder settings?

Go to Settings > Reminder Preferences to customize when and how you receive notifications.

Can I import dates from other calendars?

Yes! You can import events from Google Calendar, Outlook, and other popular calendar applications.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption and never share your personal information with third parties.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account anytime from Settings > Danger Zone. All your data will be permanently removed.
